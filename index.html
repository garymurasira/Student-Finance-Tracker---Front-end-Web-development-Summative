Purpose

This Student Finance Tracker is a responsive web application designed to help students record, organize, and analyze their daily expenses. It enables users to manage transactions, monitor spending trends, and search financial records to make better budgeting decisions. The application focuses on usability, accessibility, and reliable data management to provide a simple and practical personal finance experience.
